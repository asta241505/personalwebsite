My Film TV Shows

Here are some of my favorite movies and TV shows from different genres!

SITCOM

HOW I MET YOUR MOTHER

Synopsis: Ted Mosby recounts to his children the long and complicated journey of how he met their mother, filled with friendship, love, and hilarious moments.

Creators: Carter Bays, Craig Thomas

Stars: Josh Radnor, Jason Segel, Cobie Smulders, Neil Patrick Harris, Alyson Hannigan

YOUNG SHELDON

Synopsis: A prequel to "The Big Bang Theory," this show follows young Sheldon Cooper as he navigates childhood in East Texas, being a genius in a world that doesn’t always understand him.

Creators: Chuck Lorre, Steven Molaro

Stars: Iain Armitage, Zoe Perry, Lance Barber, Jim Parsons (narrator)

ANIME

TOKYO GHOUL

Synopsis: After an encounter with a flesh-eating ghoul, college student Kaneki Ken undergoes a transformation that forces him to live as a half-ghoul, struggling to survive in a world where ghouls and humans are enemies.

Director: Shuhei Morita

Stars: Natsuki Hanae, Sora Amamiya, Shintarō Asanuma

HUNTER X HUNTER

Synopsis: Young Gon Freecss dreams of becoming a Hunter like his long-lost father. Along the way, he makes friends, faces deadly enemies, and discovers the true dangers of the Hunter world.

Director: Hiroshi Kōjina

Stars: Megumi Han, Mariya Ise, Issei Futamata
