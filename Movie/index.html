My Kind of Movie

Here are some of my movies in different genre

SCIENCE FICTION

DIVERGENT

Synopsis: In a dystopian future, society is divided into factions based on virtues. A young woman discovers she is Divergent and must uncover the conspiracy that threatens her society.

Director: Neil Burger

Stars: Shailene Woodley, Theo James, Kate Winslet, Miles Teller

Maze Runner

Synopsis: A group of boys is trapped in a mysterious maze with no memory of the outside world, and they must find a way to escape while uncovering hidden secrets.

Director: Wes Ball

Stars: Dylan O'Brien, Kaya Scodelario, Thomas Brodie-Sangster, Will Poulter

ROMANCE

Forever My Girl

Synopsis: A country music star returns to his hometown, where he reconnects with his high school sweetheart and the daughter he never knew he had.

Director: Bethany Ashton Wolf

Stars: Alex Roe, Jessica Rothe, John Benjamin Hickey, Abby Ryder Fortson

10 Things I Hate About You

Synopsis: A modern retelling of Shakespeare's "The Taming of the Shrew," where a high schooler tries to set up his crush's ill-tempered older sister so he can date the girl he likes.

Director: Gil Junger

Stars: Heath Ledger, Julia Stiles, Joseph Gordon-Levitt, Larisa Oleynik
