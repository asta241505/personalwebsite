How to Go There

To travel from the Philippines to the USA, you can book a direct flight from Manila (NAIA) to major cities like New York, Los Angeles, or San Francisco. Airlines such as Philippine Airlines, Delta, and United offer direct flights, while other airlines may offer connecting flights with one stop. The flight duration for direct flights is typically around 13 to 15 hours, depending on the destination city in the USA.

What I Want to See & Experience:

Statue of Liberty

I want to visit the Statue of Liberty, one of the most iconic symbols of freedom and democracy. Located on Liberty Island in New York Harbor, it stands as a welcoming beacon for immigrants arriving in the U.S. The statue's impressive size and design, along with the breathtaking views of the Manhattan skyline from the island, make it a must-see.

Brooklyn Bridge

I want to visit the Brooklyn Bridge, a historic and architectural marvel that connects Manhattan and Brooklyn. Walking across the bridge offers stunning views of the New York skyline, the East River, and the Statue of Liberty. The bridge itself, with its iconic Gothic-style arches and suspension cables, is a perfect blend of beauty and engineering.

Grand Central Station

I want to visit Grand Central Station, one of New York’s most iconic landmarks. Its stunning Beaux-Arts architecture, with the grand celestial dome and ornate details, makes it a must-see. The station’s hustle and bustle, combined with its historic charm, gives it a unique energy. Time Square

I want to visit Times Square, the heart of New York’s vibrant energy and excitement. Known as "The Cross-roads of the World," it’s famous for its bright billboards, neon lights, and constant buzz. Whether it’s day or night, Times Square has an electrifying atmosphere that’s hard to miss. I’m looking forward to experiencing the crowds, the street performers, and the incredible sense of being in the middle of it all.

Central Park

I want to visit Central Park, a peaceful escape in the middle of New York City’s hustle and bustle. With its expansive green spaces, scenic walking paths, and beautiful lakes, it’s the perfect place to relax and enjoy nature. I’m excited to explore its landmarks like Bethesda Terrace, Strawberry Fields, and the Central Park Zoo.
