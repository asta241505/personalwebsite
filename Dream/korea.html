Korea

I want to visit Korea for its delicious food, rich culture, and exciting attractions. From K-dramas and K-pop spots to traditional palaces and modern shopping streets, there’s so much to explore. I’m excited to try street food, visit aesthetic cafés, and experience the vibrant nightlife. Korea’s mix of history, technology, and entertainment makes it a dream destination!

How to Go There:

To get to Korea, the most common way is by flying into Incheon International Airport (ICN) in Seoul, which is the country's main international airport. There are direct flights to Seoul from most major cities around the world. Once in Seoul, you can easily navigate the city using public transport such as the subway, buses, or taxis. You can also take high-speed trains like the KTX to reach other cities such as Busan or Daegu.

What I Want to See & Experience:

Authentic Samgyup

One of the top reasons I want to visit Korea is to experience authentic samgyupsal (Korean BBQ) in its homeland. The thought of grilling fresh, high-quality pork belly right at the table, wrapping it in crisp lettuce with ssamjang, garlic, and kimchi, is something I can’t wait to try. I also want to experience the lively atmosphere of Korean BBQ restaurants, where friends and family gather to enjoy delicious food together.

Seoul Tower

Visiting Seoul Tower is a must for me because it offers a breathtaking panoramic view of the entire city. Standing at the top and seeing Seoul's skyline, especially at night, feels like a magical experience. I also want to visit the famous Love Locks area, where couples leave padlocks as a symbol of their love. Aside from the stunning views, I’m excited to explore the observatory, enjoy the vibrant atmosphere, and experience the romantic and iconic landmark that represents the beauty of Seoul.

Try Hanbok at Gyeongbokgung Palace

Wearing a hanbok at Gyeongbokgung Palace is something I’ve always wanted to experience because it allows me to feel connected to Korea’s rich history and culture. The palace itself is a stunning masterpiece, with its grand architecture and beautiful courtyards, making it the perfect place to take photos while dressed in traditional Korean attire. Plus, wearing a hanbok gives free entry to the palace, making the experience even more special.

Nami Island

Visiting Nami Island is a dream because of its breathtaking natural beauty and peaceful atmosphere. The island is famous for its stunning tree-lined paths, especially during autumn when the leaves turn vibrant shades of red, orange, and yellow, creating a magical scene. It’s also a well-known filming location for K-dramas, making it a must-visit spot for any K-drama fan. I’m excited to take a relaxing stroll, enjoy the scenic views, and experience the romantic and tranquil charm that makes Nami Island one of Korea’s most beautiful destinations.

Myeongdong Shopping Street

I want to visit Myeongdong Shopping Street because it is one of the best shopping districts in Korea, filled with trendy fashion, beauty stores, and delicious street food. As a shopping lover, I’m excited to explore the countless shops offering the latest K-fashion, skincare products, and unique Korean cosmetics.
