My Bucket List

Here are some of the experiences I want to achieve during my lifetime. From traveling to amazing destinations, trying new activities, and accomplishing personal goals, this list will guide me through my journey of dreams!

Aurora Borealis

Witness the magical northern lights dancing across the Arctic sky in places like Iceland or Norway.

Go to Maldives

Relax in luxurious overwater bungalows and enjoy the crystal-clear waters and vibrant marine life.

Learn Surfing

Take lessons and catch your first wave at a beach destination like Siargao or Bali.

To Dine Near the Hanoi Train Station

Savor local Vietnamese delicacies at vibrant street-side eateries near the historic train station.

Take a pic with Glico Man

Snap a photo with the iconic neon sign in Osaka’s Dotonbori district.

Skydiving

Experience the thrill of freefalling from the sky and take in stunning aerial views.

Attend Coachella

Enjoy live music, vibrant art installations, and the festival atmosphere in California’s iconic Coachella Valley.

See Cherry Blossom in Japan

Witness the breathtaking sakura bloom and experience the beauty of Japan's iconic cherry blossoms.

Go to Siargao

Explore stunning beaches, surf the famous Cloud 9, and relax in a laid-back island paradise.

Visit the Blue Lagoon in Iceland

Relax in the rejuvenating, mineral-rich waters surrounded by black lava fields.
